14/// Sessions that have not gotten a response after this interval will be considered "soft archived"
15/// and will not be available for sending.
16///
17/// Set to 30 days to match the length clients kept pre-keys in their stores (at the time this
18/// expiration was added), but the two durations aren't actually tied together: if Alice fetches
19/// pre-keys on day 29, and Bob deletes them on day 30, Alice will still try to use them on day 32
20/// even with a shorter max session age.
21///
22/// Lower values result in senders resetting their sessions more often (by fetching new pre-keys
23/// from the server). Higher values result in a higher likelihood that the sender will send a
24/// message the receiver no longer has the keys to process.
25pub const MAX_UNACKNOWLEDGED_SESSION_AGE: Duration = Duration::from_secs(60 * 60 * 24 * 30);
